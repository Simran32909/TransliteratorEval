pākatantram ājyabhāgāntaṃ kṛtvā purastād agneḥ pratīcīṃ gāṃ dhārayitvā paścād agneḥ prāṅmukha upaviśya kartā śāntyudakaṃ karoti
vaśāyā anvārabdhāyai
kāṣṭhena tṛṇena vā ya ātmadā iti sūktaṃ śāntyudake 'nuyojayet
mātalyantena śāntyudakenācāmayati ca samprokṣati ca vaśām
kartordhvasthitaḥ
vāstoṣpatyādicaturgaṇīmahāśāntim uccair abhinigadati
vaśām abhimukhasthitaḥ kartā
ya īśe paśupatiḥ iti sūktenājyaṃ hutvā tato vaśāyāḥ śirasi anakti kakude skandhe jaghanadeśe
churikāyāṃ paścādanakti
dakṣiṇe pārśve darbhābhyāṃ dvābhyām adhikṣipati prajāpataye tvādhikṣipāmi ityanena mantreṇa
niḥsālām iti sūktenolmukena triḥ pradakṣiṇaṃ paribhrāmayitvā paśuṃ madhye kṛtvātmānaṃ hi
dvābhyāṃ darbhābhyāṃ vaśāmanvālabhya
paścād uttarato 'gner vaśāṃ nītvā tata ekaṃ darbhaṃ sam asyai iti mantreṇa bhūmau kṛtvā tata upari vaśāṃ pātayati pratyakśīrṣīm udakpādīṃ nividhyati
tataḥ samidhādhvaryur vaśāmukhaṃ nirodhayati nirucchvāsaṃ karoti mārayati prajānantaḥ ityṛcā
dakṣiṇatas tiṣṭhan rakṣohaṇam anuvākaṃ japati
yad vaśā māyum ityṛcā kalpajayājyaṃ juhoti saṃjñaptāyām
tataḥ patny udapātraṃ gṛhītvā paśusamīpaṃ gacchati
tataḥ paśormukhādīni gātrāṇi prakṣālayati
mukhaṃ śundhasva devayajyāyā iti mantraṃ kartā brūyāt
prāṇān śundhasva devayajyāyā iti prāṇān
nāsike śundhasveti nāsike
cakṣuṣī śundhasveti cakṣuṣī
śrotraṃ śundhasveti śrotram
karṇau śundhasveti karṇau
yat te krūraṃ yadāsthitam iti samantaṃ rajjudhānam
caritrāṇīti mantreṇa pādān samāharati
nābhiṃ śundhasva devayajyāyā iti nābhim
meḍhraṃ śundhasveti meḍhram
pāyuṃ śundhasveti pāyum pratimantraṃ gātraprakṣālanam
śeṣamudakaṃ pārśvadeśe nikṣipya yat te krūraṃ yadāsthitaṃ tacchundhasveti mantreṇa tato patnī yathārthaṃ vrajati
tato vapāśrapaṇyāvājyaṃ sruvaṃ svadhitiṃ darbham ādāya paśusthāne gacchati
parivartmānulomaṃ tato nābhideśe darbham āstṛṇāti
oṣadhe trāyasvainam iti churikāṃ prayacchati
idam ahaṃ mahumadasya bhūtikarṇaputrasyety anena mantreṇa darbhasahitaṃ nābhideśaṃ chinatti
idam aham iti mantreṇāvaradarbhakhaṇḍaṃ lohitaliptam āsyasthāne 'pahanti
nikhanatītyarthaḥ
vapayā dyāvāpṛthivī ityanena vapāśrapaṇyau vapayā pracchādya svadhitinā prakṛtyotkṛtya tataś chedanasthānaṃ ghṛtenābhighārya
śāntavṛkṣamayāv ekaikaśṛṅgā dvitīyā dviśṛṅgā
