oṃ akāro dakṣiṇaḥ pakṣa ukāras tūttaraḥ smṛtaḥ
makāras tasya pucchaṃ vā ardhamātrā śiras tathā
pādau rajas tamas tasya śarīraṃ sattvam ucyate
dharmaś ca dakṣiṇaṃ cakṣur adharmaś cottaraṃ smṛtam
bhūr lokaḥ pādayos tasya bhuvarlokas tu jānunoḥ
svar lokaḥ kaṭideśe tu nābhideśe mahar jagat
janalokas tu hṛdaye kaṇṭhadeśe tapas tataḥ
bhruvor lalāṭamadhye tu satyaloko vyavasthitaḥ
sahasram iti cātra mantra eva pradarśitaḥ
evam enaṃ samārūḍho haṃsaṃ yogavicakṣaṇaḥ
na badhyate karmacārī pāpakoṭiśatair api
āgneyī prathamā mātrā vāyavyaiṣā vaśānugā
bhānumaṇḍalasaṃkāśā bhaven mātrā tathottarā
paramā cārdhamātrā ca vāruṇīṃ tāṃ vidur budhāḥ
kalātrayānanā vāpi tāsāṃ mātrāpratiṣṭhitā
eṣa oṃkāra ākhyāto dhāraṇābhir nibodhata
yoṣiṇī prathamā mātrā vidyunmālā tathāparā
pataṃgī ca tṛtīyā syāc caturthī vāyuveginī
pañcamī nāmadheyā ca ṣaṣṭhī caindrī vidhīyate
saptamī vaiṣṇavī nāma śaṃkarī ca tathāṣṭamī
navamī mahatī nāma dhruveti daśamī matā
ekādaśī bhaven maunī brāhmīti dvādaśī matā
prathamāyāṃ tu mātrāyāṃ yadi prāṇair viyujyate
sa rājā bhārate varṣe sārvabhaumaḥ prajāyate
dvitīyāyāṃ samutkrānto bhaved yakṣo mahātmavān
vidyādharas tṛtīyāyāṃ gandharvas tu caturthikām
pañcamyām atha mātrāyāṃ yadi prāṇair viyujyate
uṣitaḥ sahadevatvaṃ somaloke mahīyate
ṣaṣṭhyām indrasya sāyujyaṃ saptamyāṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ padam
aṣṭamyāṃ vrajate rudraṃ paśūnāṃ ca patiṃ tathā
navamyāṃ ca maharlokaṃ daśamyāṃ ca dhruvaṃ vrajet
ekādaśyāṃ tapolokaṃ dvādaśyāṃ brahma śāśvatam
tataḥ paraṃ śuddhaṃ vyāpakaṃ niṣkalaṃ śivam
sadoditaṃ paraṃ brahma jyotiṣām udayo yataḥ
atīndriyaṃ guṇātītaṃ mano līnaṃ yadā bhavet
anaupamyam abhāvaṃ ca yogayuktaṃ tadādiśet
tadbhaktas tatsamāsaktaḥ śanair muñcet kalevaram
susthito yogacāreṇa sarvasaṅgavivarjitaḥ
tato vilīnapāśo 'sau vimalaḥ kevalaḥ prabhuḥ
tenaiva brahmabhāvena paramānandam aśnute
iti
