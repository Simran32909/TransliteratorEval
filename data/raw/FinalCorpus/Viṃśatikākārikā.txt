vijñaptimātramevedamasadarthāvabhāsanāt
yadvat taimirikasyāsatkeśoṇḍrakādidarśanam
na deśakālaniyamaḥ saṃtānāniyamo na ca
na ca kṛtyakriyā yuktā vijñaptiryadi nārthataḥ
deśādiniyamaḥ siddhaḥ svapnavat pretavatpunaḥ
saṃtānāniyamaḥ sarvaiḥ pūyanadyādidarśane
svapnopaghātavatkṛtyakriyā narakavatpunaḥ
narakapālādidarśane taiśca bādhane
tiraścāṃ saṃbhavaḥ svarge yathā ca narake tathā
na pretānāṃ yatastajjaṃ duḥkhaṃ nānubhavanti te
yadi tatkarmabhistatra bhūtānāṃ saṃbhavastathā
iṣyate pariṇāmaśca kiṃ vijñānasya neṣyate
karmaṇo vāsanānyatra phalamanyatra kalpyate
tatraiva neṣyate yatra vāsanā kiṃ nu kāraṇam
rūpādyāyatanāstitvaṃ tadvineyajanaṃ prati
abhiprāyavaśāduktamupapādukasattvavat
nāstīha sattva ātmā vā dharmāstvete sahetukāḥ
yataḥ svabījād vijñaptiryadābhāsā pravartate
dvividhāyatanatvena te tasyā munirabravīt
tathā pudgalanairātmyapraveśo hi anyathā punaḥ
deśanā dharmanairātmyapraveśaḥ kalpitātmanā
na tadekaṃ na cānekaṃ viṣayaḥ paramāṇuśaḥ
na ca te saṃhatā yasmātparamāṇurna sidhyati
ṣaṭkena yugapadyogātparamāṇoḥ ṣaḍaṃśatā
ṣaṇṇāṃ samānadeśatvātpiṇḍaḥ syādaṇumātrakaḥ
paramāṇorasaṃyoge tatsaṃghāte'sti kasya saḥ
na cānavayavatvena tatsaṃyogānna sidhyati
digbhāgabhedo yasyāsti tasyaikatvaṃ na yujyate
chāyāvṛtī kathaṃ vā anyo na piṇḍaś cen na tasya te
ekatve na krameṇetir yugapan na grahāgrahau
vicchinnānekavṛttiśca sūkṣmānīkṣā ca no bhavet
pratyakṣabuddhiḥ svapnādau yathā sā ca yadā tadā
na so'rtho dṛśyate tasya pratyakṣatvaṃ kathaṃ matam
uktaṃ yathā tadābhāsā vijñaptiḥ smaraṇaṃ tataḥ
svapnadṛgviṣayābhāvaṃ nāprabuddho 'vagacchati
anyonyādhipatitvena vijñaptiniyamo mithaḥ
middhenopahataṃ cittaṃ svapne tenāsamaṃ phalam
maraṇaṃ paravijñaptiviśeṣādvikriyā yathā
smṛtilopādikānyeṣāṃ piśācādimanovaśāt
kathaṃ vā daṇḍakāraṇyaśūnyatvamṛṣikopataḥ
manodaṇḍo mahāvadyaḥ kathaṃ vā tena sidhyati
paracittavidāṃ jñānamayathārthaṃ kathaṃ yathā
svacittajñānam ajñānādyathā buddhasya gocaraḥ
vijñaptimātratāsiddhiḥ svaśaktisadṛśī mayā
kṛteyaṃ sarvathā sā tu na cintyā buddhagocaraḥ
