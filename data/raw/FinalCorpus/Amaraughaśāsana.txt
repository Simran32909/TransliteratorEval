atha daśamadvāraṃ dvividhaṃ śukramārgam amṛtaṃ kālamārgaś ceti brahmadaṇḍamūle raviśaśimadhye bhagākāram asti tasmād āgatabrahmadaṇḍāśritaṃ paścimaliṅgam asti paścimaśabdena sthānam asti tasya madhye liṅgākāram asti
puruṣāṇāṃ retomārgaḥ strīṇāṃ rajomārgaḥ sahaiva tena brahmadaṇḍarekhāśritapuṣpasamaye sarvavyāpakanāḍīsamūhāgataṃ kāminīrajaḥ sravati
tatraiva kāmaviṣaharanirañjanānāṃ saṃyogaṃ bījapātāt ānandāgamaḥ pralayakālaviṣakālayoḥ kartā nirañjanaś ca iti
