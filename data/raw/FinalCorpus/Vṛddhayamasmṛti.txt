nāradād yāś ca munayo yamaṃ dharmabhṛtāṃ varam
praṇamya sukham āsīnaṃ vṛddhaṃ jñānavidāṃ varam
papracchur vaidikaṃ karma sarvalokopakārakam
sarvapāpakṣayakaraṃ śravaṇājñānamahat
maharṣe sampravakṣyāmi yamo na tvātrilocanam
saṃgṛhya dharmaśāstrāṇi sarvalokopakārakam
suptotthāya uṣaḥ prātaḥ ācamyāthaharaṃ smaret
utthāyanair ṛte dese malamūtre visarjayet
grāmāhur ataraṅgatvā tṛṇair ācchādya medinīm
śiraḥ prāvṛtya vastreṇa pidhāyāsyaṃ kareṇa ca
uttarādimukhaḥ kuryāt saṃdhyayor malamūtrakam
divottarāmukhaḥ kuryād rātrau yāmyahariṅmukhaḥ
pratyaṅmukhas tu pūrvāhṇe parāhṇe haridiṇmukhaḥ
na kedāre na setau ca nāśucau na ca bhūruhe
gaṇḍūṣayen mūtrayed vā nadīdevālayāntike
dantaśuddhiṃ ca yaḥ kuryāt pacyate narakādinā
gṛhītaśiśnaś cotthāya maunīvastrāvakuṇṭhitaḥ
pūrvam uddhṛtatoyena śaucaṃ kuryāt tathā mṛdā
na mārgasthānavalmīkānoṣarasthākhusaṃcitāt
nānyaśiṣṭāṃ ca nārdrāṃ ca śaucārthaṃ mṛdam āharet
gudeṣṭābhir dvādaśabhiḥ mṛdbhiḥ ṣoḍaśabhiś ca vā
caturviṃśatibhiś cāpi dvātriṃśadbhir athāpi vā
nyūnādhikān akartavyā asaṃkhyāś ca vṛthā bhavet
yāvad gandhakṣayo vāpi śaucaṃ kuryād viśuddhaye
ādyantaśodhanaṃ kuryāt śaucasthalamaharniśam
nocet pitṝn bhakṣayati svayaṃ cāśucitāṃ vrajet
liṅgeti saḥ kare vāme daśāsapta ca dakṣiṇe
pādayoḥ sapta vā tisro mṛdāmalakamātrayā
aivaṃ malavisarge tu mūtraśauce tu kathyate
ekāliṅge kare tisraḥ dve vā pañca ca hastayoḥ
savyahastaṃ bahir jānau apaśyaiva tad antarā
evaṃ śocaṃ dvijaḥ kṛtvā ājānukṣālayet pade
śaucamitthaṃ gṛhasthasya varṇī tu dviguṇaṃ caret
triguṇaṃ tu vanasthasya yatīnāṃ tu caturguṇam
strīśūdrāṇāṃ yathā śocaṃ sarveṣāṃ ca manaḥ śuciḥ
kṛtvā dvādaśagaṇḍūṣaṃ malasūtre tu ṣaḍ bhavet
praveśayet karaṃ savyaṃ jale cāmaṇi bandhanam
tata ādāyahastena prāṅmukhaḥ salilaṃ pibet
samantrakam aśabdaṃ ca hṛdayasthe jale punaḥ
aṅguṣṭhānābhikābhyaṃ ca dvādaśāṅgāni saṃspṛśet
madhye madhye jalaṃ spṛśya tattad evaṃ su cintayet
parimṛśya dvir āsyaṃ tu sakṛd oṣṭhau parispṛśet
vedatrayaṃ samuccāryam āṣamātrajalaṃ pibet
tvaṃ nyūnam adhikaṃ pītaṃ sureti kavayo viduḥ
vyāhṛtībhiś caturvāraṃ triḥ pibet praṇavena vā
sāvitryā va pibed ambū triḥ pibed vāpy āmantrakam
gokarṇavat karaṃ kṛtvā gṛhṇīyād devatīrthataḥ
pibe ca brahmatīrthe na divā vīkṣya jalaṃ pibet
pibed vipro jale hyasthe karmasthe bāhujaḥ punaḥ
vaiśyāśūdrās tathā nārya āsyasthe ca punāti ca
pavitraṃ dhārayan vipraḥ śuddhācamanam ācaret
vaidhekarmaṇi matyājyaṃ pavitraṃ tv anyathātmajet
brahmagandhiṃ karmakāle bhuktikāle ca vartulam
catubhir darbhapiñjūlair brāhmaṇasya pavitrakam
ekaikaṃ nyūnam uddiṣṭaṃ varṇe varṇe yathā kramam
dantaśuddhiṃ tataḥ kuryād dantakāṣṭhaiḥ śubhaiḥ samaiḥ
kṣīravṛkṣodbhavaiḥ kuryād yad vā kaṇṭakavṛkṣakaiḥ
kāghāsaiḥ kramukaiḥ kāśaiḥ kharguraś ca niṣiddhajaiḥ
na kurddantasi śuddhyaṃ ca kṛtvā sadyo tyajo bhavet
prādakṣiṇyā krameṇaiva mandayantrena kārayet
pratīpad bhūtaṣaṣṭhīṣu navabhyām athaparvasu
śrāddhāhe dantaśuddhiś ced dahaty āsaptamakulam
saptamyāmasitārkeṣu śravaṇe janmabhedathā
na kuryād dantaśudhyaṃ tau gaṇḍūṣānddādaśācaret
aindre sampadam āpnoti aiśānyāṃ sarvasiddhayaḥ
itaratra mahān doṣaḥ putrastrīpaśunāśanaḥ
evaṃ dantānūśucī kṛtvā punar ācamya buddhimān
saṃkalpya vidhipūrvaṃ tu tataḥ snātum upakramet
