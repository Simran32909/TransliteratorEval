ananyajasaptatantau anutarṣasvīkaraṇasyātyāvaśyakatvāt
ratyānandasyodbodhe anirvacanīyānandasya kāraṇabhūtatvāt
udañjyabhyutthāne pracetasaḥ kanyāyāḥ svīkaraṇasya paramakāraṇatvam
prayojyaprādhānyakartṛtvābhāve ratitantraṃ vidhātum aśakyatvāt
prayojye narmavyāpāravistāre abhyutthānaviśiṣṭodañjyabhāve sarvāṅgīṇavyāpāropalakṣitaṃ ratitantraṃ kartum adhikārābhāvāt
ratitantravilāse anirvacanīyarasotpattau sīdhusaṃgrahaṇasya paramakāraṇatvam
lalitavibhramabandhavilāse puṣkarādhipater ātmajāyāḥ naisargikasvabhāvaḥ
upaśyāmayā saha madhuvāra udañjidhārṣṭye prayojakaḥ
śyāmayā saha nirveśanaṃ yāmadvayasopalakṣitadiṣṭāvadhiretaḥstambhane paramakāraṇam
nistanūruhavarāṅgasambhede yoṣāyā anuprāśanasyātyāvaśyakatvāt
kriyamāṇe 'nuprāśane nirbhedavyathā nānubhūyate
uttarasmin ghasre asaṃbheditam iva varāṅgaṃ paridṛśyate
kriyamāṇe narmavyāpāre udañjer dhārṣṭyasya phalegrahiḥ paridṛśyate
upaśyāmayā saha vyānatādibandhe samupasthitau pāśina ātmajāyāḥ svīkaraṇe anirvacanīyasukhānubhave hetuḥ kāraṇatvam
aprāptayauvanābhiḥ saha samprayoge aṇumātraprāśanena caritārthatvāt
tīyapratyayasya prakṛtibhūtāyām avasthāyāṃ kaśyasvīkaraṇasyātyāvaśyakatvaṃ sati pūrvarūpasaṃyoge
taditarāvasthāyāṃ tu nādhikārakatvam
ekatra parijñānābhāvāt
itaratra pāñcabhautikāvayavānāṃ kṣīṇataratvāc ca
haripriyāvatāṃ janānām etac chākheṇānuvartanam atyāvaśyakam
taditareṣām etacchākhe pravṛttir na vidhīyate
pāriśeṣyāj jaladhisambhavayā yukteṣv etacchāstrasya pravṛttiḥ
gotrāpatyaghasre anutarṣasvīkaraṇasyātyāvaśyakatvam iti mārkaṇḍeyādiḥ
kāvyaghasreṣv atyāvaśyakatamam iti surathavaiśyau
vākyadvayasya śaktyupāsanāvatāṃ viniyogāt
taditareṣāṃ yathākālopadeśaḥ
dvitīyavarṇe sārvakālikam abhyanujñānam
yāgahetunā prathamavarṇasya yathāvācanikābhyanujñānam
khaṇḍamaṇḍalādhipatyādīnām indirāvatāṃ janānām api sīdhugrahaṇasya vilāsaḥ sārvakālikaḥ
svakīyapriyasāhacaryeṇa yuvatīnām abhyanujñānam
tatrāpy ananyajamakheṣv eva netaratra vidhānam
yoṣāyāḥ āsyapadmena prāśanaṃ bhāṣāyāḥ prabodhe avyabhicaritakāraṇam
śyāmāyā āsyasahasrapattrād anuprāśanam aparokṣānubhavena mūlaprakṛteḥ svarūpasyānudarśanam
