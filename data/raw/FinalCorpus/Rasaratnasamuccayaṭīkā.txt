hiṅgulaṃ haritālaṃ ca gandhakaṃ ca manaḥśilā
eṣāṃ gandhāpahāraṃ yat kurute tacca vedhakam
athāgnibaddhaṃ pāradamāha kevala iti
ādau ṣaḍguṇenābhrakajāraṇenāgnisahaḥ pārado maṇijīrṇaḥ pātanāyantreṇotthāpitaḥ sa druto bhavati
sa ca kevalastīkṣṇalohayukto vā dhmātaḥ san guṭikākṛtiścāgnāvakṣayaśca bhavati
ata evāyam agnibaddha ityucyate
ayaṃ mukhe dhṛtaścet puruṣasyākāśagāmitvaśastrāgnyabhedyatvasāyujyasārūpyādimuktikaro bhavet
mahābandhamāha hemnā veti
atra hema caturthāṃśam ardhāṃśaṃ vā grāhyam
rajatamapi tathaiva
bandhauṣadhiliptavajramūṣāmadhye tayor anyatareṇa yukto dhmāto'gnibaddha eva pārado vakṣyamāṇalakṣaṇaviśiṣṭaḥ susaṃskṛtakṛtasaṃjñāṃ mahābandhasaṃjñāṃ ca labhate
tānyeva lakṣaṇāny āha dhmāto vrajatyekatām ityādinā
paṭuvat
saindhavatulyaṃ cūrṇatvaṃ yāti
nihataḥ saṃdravati
agniyogāditi śeṣaḥ
parpaṭīrasamāha rasaṃ dviguṇeti
sabhṛṅgakaṃ bhṛṅgarājarasena mardayitvā taptaṃ drāvitaṃ tadgomayoparisthe kadale snigdhayā lohadarvyā prakṣipya tadupari kadalīdale nyubje gomayaṃ dattvā karatalādinā nipīḍya parpaṭākāraṃ kuryāt
kadalīdalasthāne snigdhe lohapatre vinikṣiptā cellohaparpaṭikā bhavet
athavā tāmrapatre snigdhe vinikṣiptā tāmraparpaṭikā bhavet
rasopari tu kadalīdalādyeva deyam
