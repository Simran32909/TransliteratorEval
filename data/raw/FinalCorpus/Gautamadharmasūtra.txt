putrā aurasakṣetrajadattakṛtrimagūḍhotpannāpaviddhā rikthabhājaḥ
kānīnasahoḍhapaunarbhavaputrikāputrasvayaṃdattakrītā gotrabhājaḥ
caturthāṃśina aurasādyabhāve
brāhmaṇasya rājanyāputro jyeṣṭho guṇasampannas tulyabhāk
jyeṣṭhāṃśahīnam anyat
rājanyāvaiśyāputrasamavāye yathā sabrāhmaṇīputreṇa
kṣatriyāc cet
śūdrāputro 'pyanapatyasya śuśrūṣuś cellabhate vṛttimūlam antevāsividhinā
savarṇāputro 'pyanyāyyavṛtto na labhetaikeṣām
śrotriyā brāhmaṇasyānapatyasya rikthaṃ bhajeran
rājetareṣām
jaḍaklībau bhartavyau
apatyaṃ jaḍasya bhāgārham
śūdrāputravat pratilomāsu
udakayogakṣemakṛtānneṣv avibhāgaḥ
strīṣu ca saṃyuktāsu
anājñāte daśāvaraiḥ śiṣṭair ūhavidbhir alubdhaiḥ praśastaṃ kāryam
catvāraś caturṇāṃ pāragā vedānāṃ prāg uttamāttraya āśramiṇaḥ pṛthag dharmavidas traya etān daśāvarān pariṣadityācakṣate
asambhave tveteṣāṃ śrotriyo vedavicchiṣṭo vipratipattau yad āha
yato 'yam aprabhavo bhūtānāṃ hiṃsānugrahayogeṣu
dharmiṇāṃ viśeṣeṇa svargaṃ lokaṃ dharmavid āpnoti jñānābhiniveśābhyām
iti dharmo dharmaḥ
