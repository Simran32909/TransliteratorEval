asataḥ satkriyāguṇavyapadeśābhāvād arthāntaram
saccāsat
yaccānyat satastadapyasat
asaditi bhūtapratyakṣābhāvād bhūtasmṛtervirodhipratyakṣatvācca jñānam
tathābhāve bhāvapratyakṣatvācca
etenāghaṭo 'gaur adharmaśca vyākhyātaḥ
abhūtaṃ nāstītyanarthāntaram
nāsti ghaṭo geha iti sato ghaṭasya gehasaṃyogapratiṣedhaḥ
nāstyanyaścandramā iti sāmānyāccandramasaḥ pratiṣedhaḥ
sadasator vaidharmyāt kārye sadasattā na
ātmanyātmamanasoḥ saṃyogaviśeṣādātmapratyakṣam
tathā dravyāntareṣu
ātmendriyamano'rthasannikarṣācca
tatsamavāyāt karmaguṇeṣu
ātmasamavāyādātmaguṇeṣu
asyedaṃ kāryaṃ kāraṇaṃ sambandhi ekārthasamavāyi virodhi ceti laiṅgikam
etena śābdaṃ vyākhyātam
heturapadeśo liṅgaṃ nimittaṃ pramāṇaṃ kāraṇamityanarthāntaram
asyedamiti buddhyapekṣatvāt
ātmamanasoḥ saṃyogaviśeṣāt saṃskārācca smṛtiḥ
tathā svapnaḥ svapnāntikaṃ ca
dharmācca
indriyadoṣāt saṃskārāccāvidyā
tadduṣṭaṃ jñānam
aduṣṭaṃ vidyā
ārṣaṃ siddhadarśanaṃ ca dharmebhyaḥ
ātmasamavāyaḥ sukhaduḥkhayoḥ pañcabhyo 'rthāntaratve hetustadāśrayibhyaśca guṇebhyaḥ
iṣṭāniṣṭakāraṇaviśeṣād virodhācca mithaḥ sukhaduḥkhayor arthāntarabhāvaḥ
saṃśayanirṇayayorarthāntarabhāvaśca jñānāntaratve hetuḥ
tayorniṣpattiḥ pratyakṣalaiṅgikābhyāṃ jñānābhyāṃ vyākhyātā
bhūtamiti pratyakṣaṃ vyākhyātam
bhaviṣyatīti kāryāntare dṛṣṭatvāt
tathā bhavatīti sāpekṣebhyo'napekṣebhyaśca
abhūdityabhūtāt
sati ca kāryāsamavāyāt
ekārthasamavāyiṣu kāraṇāntareṣu darśanādekadeśa ityekasmin
śiraḥ pṛṣṭhamudaraṃ pāṇiriti tadviśeṣebhyaḥ
kāraṇamiti dravye kāryasamavāyāt
saṃyogādvā
kāraṇasamavāyātkarmaṇi
tathā rūpe kāraṇakāraṇasamavāyācca
kāraṇasamavāyātsaṃyoge
tathā kāraṇākāraṇasamavāyācca
saṃyuktasamavāyādagnervaiśeṣikam
laiṅgikaṃ pramāṇaṃ vyākhyātam
dṛṣṭānāṃ dṛṣṭaprayojanānāṃ dṛṣṭābhāve prayogo 'bhyudayāya
tadvacanād āmnāyaprāmāṇyamiti
