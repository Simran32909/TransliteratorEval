śrīgaṇeśagīrbhyāṃ namaḥ
lambodaraṃ mahākāyaṃ lambauṣṭhaṃ gajakarṇakam
vighnaghnaṃ pārvvatīputraṃ namāmi gaṇanāyakam
vinā śastradharaṃ nānyo dhanurvvedasya pāragaḥ
ataḥ svapne niśi prāpte śivatattvavicāraṇam
ataḥ mandehadolāyāṃ ropaṇīyaṃ na mānasam
granthe 'smin cāpacaturair vīracintāmaṇau kvacit
yasyābhyāse prasādena niṣpadyante dhanurdharāḥ
jetāraḥ parasainyānāṃ tasyābhyāso vidhīyatām
eko'pi yatra nagare prasiddhaḥ syāddhanurddharaḥ
tato yāntyarayo dūraṃ mṛgāḥ siṃhagṛhādiva
ācāryyeṇa dhanurdeyaṃ brāhmaṇena parīkṣite
śaṭhe dhūrtte kṛtaghne ca mandabuddhau na dīyate
brāhmaṇāya dhanurdeyaṃ khaḍgaṃ vai kṣatriyāya ca
vaiśyāya dāpayetkuntaṃ gadāṃ śūdrāya dāpayet
dhanuścakraṃ tu kuntaṃ ca khaḍgaṃ ca churikāṃ gadām
saptamaṃ bāhūyuddhaṃ syādevaṃ yuddhāni saptadhā
ācāryyaḥ saptayuddhaiḥ syāccaturbhirbhārgavastathā
dvābhyāṃ caiva bhavedyoddhā ekena gaṇako bhavet
hastaḥ punarvasuḥ puṣyo rohiṇī cottarātrayam
anurādhāśvinī caiva revatī daśamī tathā
janmasthe ca tṛtīye ca ṣaṣṭhe vai saptame tathā
daśamaikādaśe candre sarvakarmāṇi kārayet
tṛtīyā pañcamī caiva saptamī navamī tathā
trayodaśī dvādaśī ca tithayaśca śubhā matāḥ
sūryavāraḥ śukravāro guruvārastathaiva ca
etad vāratrayaṃ dhanyaṃ prārambhe śastrakarmaṇi
ebhir dinaiśca śiṣyāya guruḥśastrāṇi dāpayet
saṃtarpya dānahomābhyāṃ surān śāstravidhānataḥ
brāhmaṇān bhojayettatra kumārīścāpyanekaśaḥ
tāpasānarcayedbhaktyā ye cānye śivayoginaḥ
annapānādibhiścaiva vastrālaṅkārabhūṣaṇaiḥ
gandhamālyair vicitraiśca guruṃ tatra prapūjayet
kṛtopavāsaḥ śiṣyastu kṛtāñjaliparigrahaḥ
baddhvāñjalipuṭastatra yācayedguruto dhanuḥ
aṅganyāsas tataḥ kāryaḥ śivoktaḥ siddhim icchatā
ācāryyeṇa ca śiṣyasya pāpaghno vighnanāśanaḥ
śikhāsthāne nyasedīśaṃ bāhuyugme ca keśavam
brahmāṇaṃ nābhimadhye tu jaṅghayośca gaṇādhipam
aṅganyāse ca mantraḥ
oṃ hauṃ śikhāmadhye śaṅkarāya namaḥ
oṃ hauṃ bāhvoḥ keśavāya namaḥ
oṃ hauṃ nābhimadhye brahmaṇe namaḥ
oṃ hauṃ jaṅghayorgaṇapataye namaḥ
īdṛśaṃ kārayennyāsaṃ yena śreyo bhaviṣyati
anye'pi duṣṭasattvāśca na hiṃsanti kadācana
śiṣyāya mānuṣaṃ cāpaṃ dhanurvvedābhimantritam
kāṇḍātkāṇḍeti mantreṇa dadyādvedavidhānataḥ
prathamaṃ puṣpavedhaṃ ca phalahīnena patriṇā
tataḥ phalayutenaiva matsyavedhaṃ ca kārayet
māṃsavedhaṃ tataḥ kuryādevaṃ vedho bhavettridhā
pūrvavedhaiḥ kṛtāḥ puṃsā śarāḥ syuḥ sarvasādhakāḥ
vedhane caivamākṛṣya śarapāto yadā bhavet
pūrvadigbhāgam āśritya sadā syādvijayī sukhī
dakṣiṇe kalaho ghoro videśagamanaṃ punaḥ
paścime dhanadhānyaṃ ca sarvaṃ caivottare bhavet
aiśānyāṃ pātanaṃ duṣṭaṃ vidiśo'nyāśca śobhanāḥ
sarvapuṣṭikarāścaiva siddhidāḥ śastrakarmaṇi
evaṃ vedhatrayaṃ kṛtvā śaṅkhadundubhinisvanaiḥ
tataḥ praṇamya gurave dhanurbāṇān nivedayet
prathamaṃ pauruṣaṃ cāpaṃ yuddhacāpaṃ dvitīyakam
nijabāhubalonmānaṃ kiṃcidūnaṃ śubhaṃ bhavet
varaṃ prāṇādhiko dhanvī natu prāṇādhikaṃ dhanuḥ
dhanuṣā pīḍyamānastu dhanvī lakṣyaṃ na paśyati
ato nijabalonmānaṃ dhanuḥ syācchubhakārakam
devānāmuttamaṃ cāpaṃ tato nyūnaṃ ca mānavam
sārddhapañcamahastantu śreṣṭhaṃ cāpaṃ prakīrtitam
tad vijñeyaṃ dhanur divyaṃ śaṅkareṇa dhṛtaṃ purā
tasmāt paraśurāmeṇa tato droṇena dhīmatā
droṇādgṛhītaṃ pārthena tataḥ sātyakinā dhṛtam
kṛtayuge mahādevaḥ tretāyāṃ caiva bhārgavaḥ
dvāpare droṇavipraśca daivaṃ cāpamadhārayat
caturviṃśāṅgulo hastaś caturhastaṃ dhanur bhavet
tad bhavenmānuṣaṃ cāpaṃ sarvalakṣaṇasaṃyutam
triparvaṃ pañcaparvaṃ ca saptaparvaṃ ca kīrtitam
navaparvaṃ ca kodaṇḍaṃ caturddhā śubhadāyakam
catuḥparvaṃ ca ṣaṭparvam aṣṭaparvaṃ ca varjayet
keṣāṃcicca bhaveccāpaṃ vitastinavasaṃmitam
atijīrṇam apakvaṃ ca jñātidhṛṣṭaṃ tathaiva ca
dagdhaṃ chinnaṃ na kartavyaṃ bāhyābhyantarahastakam
guṇahīnaṃ guṇākrāntaṃ kāṇḍadoṣasamanvitam
galagranthi talagranthi na kartavyaṃ sadā budhaiḥ
apakvaṃ bhaṅgamāyāti atijīrṇaṃ tu karkaśam
jñātidhṛṣṭaṃ tu sodvegaṃ kalaho bāndhavaiḥ saha
dagdhena dahyate veśma chidraṃ buddhivināśanam
bāhye lakṣyaṃ na labhyeta tathaivābhyantare'pi ca
hīne tu saṃdhite bāṇe saṃgrāme bhaṅgakārakam
ākrānte tu punarlakṣye lakṣyaṃ na prāpyate dṛḍham
galagranthi talagranthi dhanahānikaraṃ dhanuḥ
etair doṣair vinirmuktaṃ sarvalakṣaṇakārakam
śārṅgaṃ tu vai dhanurdivyaṃ tadviṣṇoḥ paramāyudham
vitastisaptamaṃ mānaṃ nirmitaṃ viśvakarmaṇā
na svarge na ca pātāle na bhūmau kasyacitkare
taddhanurna vaśaṃ ghāti muktvaikaṃ puruṣottamam
pauruṣeyaṃ tu yat kāryaṃ bahuyatnena yojitam
vitastibhiḥ saptabhiśca mitaṃ sarvārthasādhakam
prāyo jñeyaṃ dhanuḥ śārṅgaṃ gajārohasya sādhanam
rathināṃ ca padātīnāṃ vāṃśaṃ cāpaṃ prakīrtitam
guṇānāṃ lakṣaṇaṃ vakṣye yādṛśaṃ kārayedguṇam
dhanuḥpramāṇaṃ niḥsandhiḥ kāryastriguṇatantubhiḥ
vartitaḥ syād guṇaḥ sūkṣmaḥ sarvakāryasaho yudhi
paṭṭasūtraiḥ guṇaḥ kāryaḥ kaniṣṭhāmānasaṃmitaḥ
abhāve paṭṭasūtrasya hāriṇī snāyuriṣyate
guṇārtham atha saṃgrāhyāḥ snāyavo mahiṣīgavām
tatkāle hatagokarṇacarma vā chāgalasya vā
vilomatanturūpeṇa kuryādvā guṇamuttamam
pakvavaṃśatvacā kāryo guṇastu sthaviro dṛḍhaḥ
paṭṭasūtreṇa saṃnaddhaḥ sarvakāryasaho yudhi
prāpte bhādrapade māse tvagarkasya praśasyate
tasyāstatra guṇaḥ kāryaḥ pavitraḥ sthāvaro dṛḍhaḥ
vṛttārkasūtratantūnāṃ hastā aṣṭādaśāḥ smṛtāḥ
tadvṛttaṃ triguṇaṃ kāryaṃ pramāṇo'yaṃ guṇasya ca
phalaṃ tu śuddhalohasya sadhāraṃ tīkṣṇamastakam
yojayed vajralepena sārapakṣānusārataḥ
ārāmukhaṃ kṣurapraṃ ca gopucchaṃ cārdhacandrakam
sūcīmukhaṃ ca bhallaṃ ca vatsadantaṃ suvallabham
karṇikaṃ kākatuṇḍaṃ ca tathā cānye'pyanekaśaḥ
phalāni deśadeśeṣu bhavanti bahurūpataḥ
ārāmukhenaiva carma kṣurapreṇa ca kārmukam
sūcīmukhena kavacam ardhacandreṇa mastakam
bhallena hṛdayaṃ vedhyaṃ dvibhallena tathaiva ca
lohaṃ ca kākatuṇḍena lakṣyaṃ gopucchakena ca
anyadgopucchakaṃ jñeyaṃ śuddhakāṃsyair vinirmitam
mukhena cāpi kaṇṭhena vedham aṅgulisaṃmitam
phalasya pāyanaṃ vakṣye divyauṣadhivilepanam
yena durvedhyavarmāṇi bhedayettarupattravat
pippalī saindhavaṃ kuṣṭhaṃ gomūtreṇa ca peṣayet
anena lepayecchastraṃ liptaṃ cāgnau pratāpayet
aviṣpātaṃ balāviddhaṃ pītamagnau tathauṣadham
tato nirvāpitaṃ lohaṃ tatra vedhe viśiṣyate
pañcabhir lavaṇaiḥ piṣṭaṃ madhusikthaiḥ sasarṣapaiḥ
ebhiḥ pralepayecchastraṃ liptaṃ cāgnau pratāpayet
śikhigrīvāptavarṇābhaṃ taptaśīrṣaṃ tathauṣadham
tatastu vimalaṃ jñeyaṃ pāyayecchastram uttamam
sarvalohāstu ye bāṇā nārācāste prakīrtitāḥ
pañcabhiḥ pṛthulaiḥ pakṣairyuktāḥ sidhyanti karhicit
nālikā laghavo bāṇā nālayantreṇa coditāḥ
yujyante dūrapāteṣu durge yuddheṣu te matāḥ
sthānānyaṣṭau vidheyāni yojayedbhinnakarmaṇā
sṛṣṭyaḥ pañca samākhyātā vyāyāḥ pañca prakīrtitāḥ
agratovāmapādena dakṣiṇaṃ jānu kuñcitam
ālīḍhe tu prakartakaṃ tathā caivānu kuñcitam
dakṣiṇaṃ tu purastādvā dūrapāte viśiṣyate
pādau na vistarau kāryau samau hastapramāṇataḥ
viśeṣasthānakaṃ jñeyaṃ kūṭalakṣyasya bhedane
same pāte samau pādau niṣkampaṃ ca susaṃgatau
asame ca purobhāge hastamātreṇa taṃ viduḥ
ākuñcitorū dvau tatra jānubhyāṃ dharaṇīgatau
vyadravadbhuvam āsthāya sthānakaṃ dṛḍhabhedanam
savyajānugataṃ bhūmau dakṣiṇaṃ ca sukuñcitam
savyaṃ jānu nataṃ bhūmau dakṣiṇaṃ na ca kuñcitam
agrato yatra dātavyaṃ taṃ vidyād garuḍaṃ kramam
padmāsanaṃ prasiddhaṃ tu upaviśya yathākramam
dhanvinām atra vijñeyaṃ sthānakaṃ śubhalakṣaṇam
patākā guṇamuṣṭiśca siṃhakarṇastathaiva ca
matsarī kākatuṇḍī ca yojanīyā yathākramam
dīrghā tu tarjanī yatra hyāśritāṅguṣṭhamūlakam
patākā sā ca vijñeyā nālikā dūramokṣaṇe
tarjanīṃ madhyamāmadhyam aṅguṣṭho viśate yadi
guṇamuṣṭistu sā jñeyā sthūle nārācamocaṇaiḥ
aṅguṣṭhamadhyadeśaṃ tu tarjanyagraṃ śubhaṃ sthitam
siṃhakarṇaḥ sa vijñeyaḥ dṛḍhalakṣyasya vedhane
aṅguṣṭhanakhamūle tu tarjanyagraṃ susaṃsthitam
matsarī sā ca vijñeyā sūkṣmalakṣyasya bhedane
aṅguṣṭhāgre tu tarjanyā mukhaṃ yatra niveśitam
kākatuṇḍī ca sā jñeyā sthūlalakṣyeṣu yojitā
sandhānaṃ trividhaṃ proktam adha ūrdhvaṃ samaṃ yadā
yojayet triprakāraṃ hi kāryeṣvapi yathākramam
atyantadūrapātitvaṃ same lakṣyaṃ suniścitam
dṛḍhāskoṭaṃ prakurvīta ūrdhvaṃ sandhānayojitam
kaiśikaḥ śaramūle ca śaraśūṅge ca sāttvikaḥ
śravaṇe vatsakarṇaśca grīvāyāṃ bharato bhavet
aṃsake skandhanāmā ca vyāyāḥ pañca prakīrtitāḥ
kaiśikaścitrayuddheṣu adholakṣyeṣu sāttvikaḥ
vatsakarṇaśca vijñeyaḥ bharato dṛḍhabhedane
dṛḍhabhede ca dūre ca skandhanāmānam uddiśet
lakṣyaṃ caturvidhaṃ jñeyaṃ sthiraṃ caiva calaṃ tathā
calācalaṃ dvayacalaṃ iti bhedā yathākramam
ātmānaṃ susthiraṃ kṛtvā lakṣyaṃ caiva sthiraṃ budhaḥ
vedhayet triprakāraṃ tu sthiravedhī sa ucyate
calaṃ tu vedhayedyastu ātmanā sthirasaṃsthitaḥ
calavedhīti sa prokta ācāryeṇa śivena vai
dhanvī tu calate yatra sthiralakṣye samāhitaḥ
calācalaṃ bhavettatra svapnameyam udīritam
ubhāveva calau yatra lakṣyaṃ vāpi dhanurdharaḥ
tad vijñeyaṃ dvayacalaṃ śrameṇaiva hi sādhakaḥ
śrameṇācalitaṃ lakṣyaṃ dūraṃ ca bahubhedanam
śrameṇa kaṭhinā muṣṭiḥ śīghrasādhanakāriṇī
śrameṇa citravedhitvaṃ śrameṇa prāpyate jayaḥ
tasmād gurusamīpe tu śramaḥ kāryo vijānatā
prathamaṃ vāmahastena yaḥ śramaṃ kurute naraḥ
tasya cāpakriyāsiddhir jāyate śīghrameva ca
vāmahastena saṃsiddhe paścāddakṣiṇam ārabhet
ubhābhyāṃ ca śramaṃ kuryānnārācaiśca śaraistathā
vāmenaiva śramaṃ kuryāt susiddhe dakṣiṇe kare
viśākhasthānake caiva tathā vyāye ca kaiśike
udite bhāskare lakṣyaṃ paścimāyāṃ niveśayet
aparāhṇe ca kartavyaṃ lakṣyaṃ pūrvadigāśritam
uttareṇa sadā kāryaṃ praśasyamavarodhakam
saṃgrāmeṇa vinā kāryaṃ na lakṣyaṃ dakṣiṇāmukham
ṣaṣṭikāṇḍāntare lakṣyaṃ jyeṣṭhalakṣyaṃ prakīrtitam
catvāriṃśanmadhyamaṃ ca viṃśatiśca kaniṣṭhakam
śarāṇāṃ kathitaṃ hyetannārācānām atho śṛṇu
catvāriṃśat ṣoḍaśaiva nārācānāṃ bhavettataḥ
catuḥśataiśca kāṇḍānāṃ yo hi lakṣyaṃ samabhyaset
sūryodaye cāstakāle sa jyeṣṭho dhanvināṃ bhavet
triśatairmadhyamaścaiva dviśatābhyāṃ kaniṣṭhakaḥ
lakṣyaṃ ca puruṣonmānaṃ kuryāccandrakasaṃyutam
ūrdhvavedhī bhavecchreṣṭho nābhivedhī tu madhyamaḥ
yaḥ pādavedhī lakṣyasya sa kaniṣṭhatamo bhavet
ataḥ paraṃ pravakṣyāmi śarāṇāṃ lakṣaṇaṃ śubham
sthūlaṃ caivāti sūkṣmaṃ ca hyapakvapakvabhūmijam
ādravaṃ yad viśīrṇaṃ ca varjayed īdṛśaṃ śaram
pūrṇagranthi supakvaṃ ca pāṇḍuraṃ samayāhṛtam
kaṭhinaṃ vartulaṃ kāṇḍaṃ gṛhṇīyātsupradeśajam
dvau hastau muṣṭihīnau ca dairghye sthaulye kaniṣṭhikāḥ
vidheyāḥ śaramāṇe tu candreṇākarṣayettataḥ
kaṅkahaṃsaśaśādānāṃ matsyādakrauñcakokilāḥ
gṛdhrāṇāṃ ca kuraṅgānāṃ pakṣā eteṣu śobhanāḥ
ekasyaiva śarasyaiva catuḥpakṣāṃśca kārayet
ṣaḍaṅgulapramāṇeṇa pakṣabhedaṃ na kārayet
daśāṅgulamitāḥ pakṣāḥ śārṅgacāpasya mārgaṇe
yojyā dṛḍhāścatuḥsaṃkhyāḥ saṃnaddhāḥ snāyuḥ tantubhiḥ
śarāśca trividhā jñeyāḥ strīpuṃsakanapuṃsakāḥ
agre sthūlā bhavennārī paścātsthūlo bhavennaraḥ
samaṃ napuṃsakaṃ jñeyaṃ tallakṣyārthaṃ praśasyate
dūrapātaṃ yuvatyāśca puruṣo bhedane dṛḍham
napuṃsako lakṣyabhedī śarāṇāṃ lakṣaṇaṃ viduḥ
aṣṭamī ca tathāmā ca varjanīyā caturdaśī
pūrṇimā ca dinaṃ yāvadvarjayeccāpakarmasu
akālagarjito devo durdinaṃ vā yadā bhavet
pūrvakāṇḍahate lakṣye anadhyāyaṃ pracakṣate
śramaṃ ca kurvato yatra bhujaṅgo yadi dṛśyate
athavā bhajyate cāpaṃ yadaiva śramakarmaṇi
truṭyate vā guṇo yatra prathame bāṇamokṣaṇe
śramaṃ tatra na kurvīta śastre matimatāṃ varaḥ
kriyākapālān vakṣyāmi śramasādhyān suviśrutān
yeṣāṃ vijñānamātreṇa siddhirbhavati nānyathā
prathamaṃ cāpamāropya tūlikāṃ vallayet tataḥ
sthānakaṃ tu tataḥ kuryād bāṇopari karaṃ nyaset
tolanaṃ dhanuṣaḥ sthairyaṃ kartavyaṃ vāmapāṇinā
ādānaṃ tu tataḥ kṛtvā sandhānaṃ tu tataḥ param
lakṣyādākṛṣya cāpena bhūmivedhaṃ na kārayet
namaskuryuḥ śivaṃ vighnarājaṃ guruvaraṃ raṇe
yācitavyā gurorājñā bāṇasyākarṣaṇaṃ prati
prāṇavāyuṃ prayatena bāṇena saha pūrayet
kumbhakena samākṛṣya huṃkāreṇa visarjayet
ityabhyāsakriyā kāryā dhanvinā siddhimicchatā
ṣaṇmāsāt sidhyate muṣṭiḥ śarāḥ saṃvatsareṇa tu
nārācāstasya sidhyanti yasya tuṣyenmaheśvaraḥ
puṣpavaddhārayed bāṇaṃ sarpavat pīḍayeddhanuḥ
dhanavaccintayellakṣyaṃ yadīcchetsiddhimātmanaḥ
kriyāmicchanti cācāryā dūram icchanti bhārgavāḥ
rājāno dṛṣṭim icchanti lakṣyam icchanti cetare
janānurañjanaṃ yena lakṣyaghātāt prajāyate
hīnenāpīṣuṇā tasmāt praśastaṃ lakṣyavedhanam
viśākhasthānake sthitvā samaṃ sandhānamācaret
gopucchamukhabāṇena siṃhakarṇena muṣṭinā
ākarṣet kaiśikavyāye śikhāmācālayettataḥ
pūrvāparau samau kāryau samāṃsau niścalau karau
cakṣuṣī spandayennaiva dṛṣṭiṃ lakṣye niyojayet
muṣṭinācchāditaṃ lakṣyaṃ śarasyāgre niyojayet
manomuṣṭigataṃ dhyātvā tataḥ kāṇḍaṃ visarjayet
sa ca lakṣyeṣu kartavyo lakṣye yodho jitaśramaḥ
ādānaṃ caiva tūṇīrāt sandhānaṃ karṣaṇaṃ tathā
kṣepaṇaṃ ca tvarāyukto bāṇasya kurute tu yaḥ
nityābhyāsavaśāt tasya śīghrasādhanatā bhavet
pratyālīḍhe kṛte sthāne adhaḥ sandhānamācaret
muṣṭyā patākayā bāṇaṃ strīsaṃjñaṃ dūrapātanam
darduravad upasthāya hyūrdhvaṃ sandhānamācaret
skandhavyāyena vajrasya muṣṭyā puṃmārgaṇe na ca
atyantasauṣṭhavaṃ bāhvor jāyate dṛḍhabheditā
sūcīmukhā mīnapucchā bhrāmarī ca tṛtīyakā
śarāṇāṃ gatayastisraḥ praśastāḥ kathitāḥ budhaiḥ
sūcīmukhā gatistasya sāyakasya prajāyate
patraṃ vilokitavyaṃ ca athavā hīnapatrakam
karkaśe na tu cāpena yat kṛṣyeddhīnamuṣṭinā
matsyapucchā gatistasya sāyakasya prakīrtitā
bhrāmarī kathitā hyeṣā sadbhiśca śramakarmaṇi
ṛjutve na vinā yāti kṣepyamānastu sāyakaḥ
vāmagā dakṣiṇā caiva ūrdhvagādhogatā tathā
catasro gatayaḥ proktāḥ bāṇaskhalanahetavaḥ
kampate guṇamuṣṭistu mārgaṇasya tu pṛṣṭhataḥ
saṃmukhīsyād dhanurmuṣṭistadā vāme gatirbhavet
grahaṇaṃ śithilaṃ yasya ṛjutvena vivarjitam
pārśve tu dakṣiṇaṃ yāti sāyakasya na saṃśayaḥ
ūrdhvaṃ bhaveccāpamuṣṭir guṇamuṣṭiradho bhavet
samukto mārgaṇo lakṣyādūrdhvaṃ yāti na saṃśayaḥ
ākarṣaṇe caiva bāṇasya cāpe muṣṭiradho bhavet
guṇamuṣṭir bhavedūrdhvaṃ tadādhogāminī gatiḥ
lakṣyabāṇāgradṛṣṭīṇāṃ saṃhatistu yadā bhavet
tadānīm ujjhito bāṇo lakṣyānna calati dhruvam
nirdoṣaḥ śabdahīnaśca samamuṣṭidvayojjhitaḥ
bhinatti dṛḍhabhedyāni sāyako nātra saṃśayaḥ
ākṛṣṭastejito yaśca viśuddho gāḍhamujjhitaḥ
naro nāgāśca kāyeṣu na sa tiṣṭhati mārgaṇaḥ
yasya tṛṇasamā bāṇā yat saṃdhānasamaṃ dhanuḥ
yasya prāṇasamā maurvī sa dhanvī dhanvināṃ varaḥ
ayaścarmaghaṭaścaiva mṛtpiṇḍaśca catuṣṭayam
yo bhinatti na tasyeṣur vajriṇāpi ca bādhyate
sārdhāṅgulapramāṇena lohapātrāṇi kārayet
tāni bhittvaikabāṇena dṛḍhaghātī bhavennaraḥ
caturviṃśaticarmāṇi yo bhinattīṣuṇā naraḥ
tasya bāṇo gajendrasya kāyaṃ nirbhidya gacchati
bhrāmyajjale ghaṭo vedhyaḥ cakre mṛtpiṇḍakaṃ tathā
bhramantaṃ bhedayedyastu dṛḍhabhedī sa ucyate
ayastu kākatuṇḍena carma cārāmukhena hi
mṛtpiṇḍaṃ ca ghaṭaṃ caiva vidhyetsūcīmukhena vai
bāṇabhaṅgakarāvarttaṃ kāṣṭhacchedanam eva ca
bindukaṃ caiva yugakaṃ yo vetti sa jayī bhavet
lakṣyasthāne dhṛtaṃ kāṇḍaṃ saṃmukhaṃ chedayettataḥ
kiṃcinmuṣṭiṃ vidhāya svāṃ tiryag dviphalakeṣuṇā
saṃmukhaṃ bāṇam āyāntaṃ tiryagbāṇaṃ na saṃcaret
prāptaṃ śareṇa yaśchindyād bāṇacchedī sa ucyate
kāṣṭhe 'śvakeśaṃ saṃyamya tatra baddhvā varāṭikām
hastena bhrāmyamāṇaṃ ca yo hanti sa dhanurdharaḥ
pañcaviddhaṃ nyasecchastraṃ śuddhaṃ bandhukapuṣpavat
hāstikaṃ bindukaṃ yastu citrabhedī sa ucyate
lakṣyaviddhaṃ kāṣṭhaṃ kṣipraṃ dūramūrūrddha vapuḥ sthitam
svayaṃ prāptaśaraṃ pṛṣṭhe ūrdhvapucchamukhena hi
yo hanti śarayugmena śīghrasaṃdhānayogataḥ
saḥ syāddhanurbhṛtāṃ śreṣṭhaḥ pūjitaḥ sarvapārthivaiḥ
lakṣyasthāne nyaset kāṣṭhaṃ sādraṃ gopucchasannibham
yaḥ chindyāttaṃ kṣurapreṇa kāṣṭhavedhī sa yāyate
lakṣyasthāne nyaset kāṃsyaṃ pātraṃ hastadvayāntare
tāḍayeccharkarābhistaṃ śabdaḥ saṃjāyate yadā
yatropalabhyate śabdaḥ samyak taṃ tatra cintayet
karmendriyamanoyogāllakṣyaṃ niścayatāṃ nayet
punaḥ śarkayā bhinnaṃ tāḍayecchabdahetave
tato bāṇena taṃ hanyāt tadavadhānena dhīmatā
etacca duṣkaraṃ karma bhāgyāt kasyāpi sidhyati
atha śramavidhiṃ kuryād yāvat siddhiḥ prajāyate
śrame siddhe ca varṣāsu naiva grāhyaṃ dhanuṣkare
pūrvābhyāsasya śastrāṇām avismaraṇahetave
māsadvayaṃ śramaṃ kuryāt prati varṣaṃ śaradṛtau
jāte cāśvayuje māsi navamī devatā dine
pūjayed īśvarīṃ caṇḍīṃ guruṃ śāstrāṇi vājigaḥ
viprebhyo dakṣiṇāndadyāt kumārīḥ pūjayettataḥ
devyai paśubaliṃ dadyādgītavāditramaṅgalaiḥ
śūlena pāhi no devi pāhi khaḍgeṇa cāmbike
ghaṇṭāsvanena naḥ pāhi cāpajyāniḥsvanena ca
prācyāṃ rakṣa pratīcyāṃ ca caṇḍike rakṣa dakṣiṇe
bhrāmaṇenātmaśūlasya uttarasyāṃ tatheśvari
saumyāni yāni rūpāṇi trailokye vicaranti te
yāni cātyarthaghorāṇi tai rakṣāsmān tathā bhuvam
tatastu sādhayenmantrān vedoktān āgamoditān
astrāṇāṃ karmasiddhyarthaṃ japahomavidhānataḥ
brāhmaṃ nārāyaṇaṃ śaivamaindraṃ vāyavyavāruṇe
āgneyaṃ vāpi cāstrāṇi gurudattāni sādhayet
manovākkarmabhir vyāptaṃ labdhāstreṇa śuciṣmatā
apātram asamarthaṃ ca dahantyastrāṇi puruṣam
prayogaṃ copasaṃhāraṃ yo vetti sa dhanurdharaḥ
sāmānye karmaṇi prājño naivāstrāṇi prayojayet
hastārke lāṅgalīkandaṃ gṛhītvā tasya lepataḥ
śūrasyāpi raṇe puṃso darpaṃ harati tatkṣaṇāt
gṛhītvā yoganakṣatrairapāmārgasya mūlakam
lepamātreṇa vīrāṇāṃ sarvaśastranivāraṇam
adhaḥpuṣpī śaṅkhapuṣpī lajjāluḥ girikarṇikā
nīlinī sahadevī ca patramauñjārkayostathā
viṣṇukrāntā ca sarvāsāṃ jaṭā grāhyā raverdine
baddhvā bhuje vilepād vā kāye sarvāstrāpavārakaḥ
sarvavyāghrādisattvāṇāṃ bhūtādīṇāṃ na jāyate
bhītistasya na tiryagbhyo mātaro'ṣṭau śarīrake
gṛhītaṃ hastanakṣatre cūrṇaṃ chuchundarī bhavet
tatprabhāvādgajaḥ puṃsāṃ saṃmukhenaiti niścitam
chuchandarīśrīphalasya cūrṇam ālipya gātrake
āghrāya gandhaṃ dviradaścātimatto madaṃ tyajet
puṣyārkotpāṭite mūle pūgena mukhasaṃsthite
dehaṃ sphuṭati notīkṣṇaṃ maṇḍalāgre raṇe nṝṇām
gāmbhāryā uttaraṃ mūlaṃ mukhasya saṃmukhāgatam
śastraughaṃ vārayettatra puṣyārke vidhinoddhṛtam
śubhrāyāḥ śarapuṅkhāyā jaṭā nīlī jaṭā tathā
bhuje śirasi vaktre vā sthitā śastranivārikā
bhūtāhicorabhītighnī gṛhītvā puṣyabhāskare
prathamaṃ kriyate snānaṃ śvetavastrāvṛto bhavet
śastrāṇi cāpi sampūjya rakṣāmantraṃ japettataḥ
senāpatiṃ gajārohān aśvārohāṃśca sarvataḥ
mukhyān anyānapi dhanair vastraiśca paridhāpayet
pūrvaṃ sārathimāropya rathe sajjet tataḥ svayam
catuḥśataiśca kāṇḍāṇāṃ tūṇīraṃ paripūrayet
śaṅkhaṃ cakraṃ gadāṃ śaktiṃ mudgaraṃ parighaṃ tathā
nārācaṃ paraśuṃ kuntaṃ paṭṭiśādīni saṃnyaset
narasthānagajāṣṭasya svayaṃ samaram ārohet
kaṭibaddhaikatūṇīraḥ khaḍgaśaktidhanurdharaḥ
tato 'rjunasya nāmāṇi viṣṇusmaraṇapūrvakam
japettataḥ pratiṣṭheta caturaṅgabalair yutaḥ
maṅgalaṃ bhagavān viṣṇurmaṅgalaṃ garuḍadhvajaḥ
maṅgalaṃ puṇḍarīkākṣo maṅgalāyatanaṃ hariḥ
lābhasteṣāṃ jayasteṣāṃ kutasteṣāṃ parājayaḥ
yeṣām indīvaraśyāmo hṛdayastho janārdanaḥ
arjunaḥ phālguno jiṣṇuḥ kirīṭī śvetavāhanaḥ
bībhatsur vijayaḥ kṛṣṇaḥ savyasācī dhanañjayaḥ
ityarjunasya nāmāṇi saṃsmaret prayato naraḥ
khadvayaṃ surarddhīndunetrair akṣauhiṇī matā
akṣauhiṇī pradiṣṭā ca rathāṇāṃ varmadhāriṇām
saṃkhyāgaṇitatattvajñaiḥ sahasrāṇy ekaviṃśati
uparyaṣṭau śatānyāhuḥ tathā bhūpaśca saptatiḥ
gajāṇāṃ ca parimāṇametadeva vinirdiśet
pañcaṣaṣṭisahasrāṇi ṣaṭśatāni daśopari
hayāṇāṃ ca parimāṇam etad eva suniścitam
jñeyaṃ lakṣaṃ padātīnāṃ sahasrāṇi tathā nava
śatāni trīṇi pañcāśacchūrāṇāṃ śastradhāriṇām
pañcaṣaṣṭisahasrāṇi tathāśvāṇāṃ śatāni ca
daśottarāṇi gāyanti saṃkhyātattvavido janāḥ
mahākṣauhiṇī kāryā rathākoṭibhatāḥ smṛtāḥ
saptatriṃśacca lakṣāṇi gīyante tattvavedibhiḥ
dvādaśaiva sahasrāṇi tathā śatacatuṣṭayam
proktāni navatis tadvadevameva gajā matāḥ
aśvāścatuḥkoṭimitā lakṣaṃ caikādaśaiva tu
saptatriṃśatsahasrāṇi tathā śatacatuṣṭayam
saptabhiścaiva saṃkhyātāḥ procyante pattayastathā
ṣaṭkoṭyo 'śītilakṣāṇi pañcādhikaśatānyapi
dviṣaṣṭiṃ ca sahasrāṇi tathā śatacatuṣṭayam
agre rathā gajāḥ pṛṣṭhe tatpṛṣṭhe ca padātayaḥ
pārśvayośca hayāḥ kāryā vyūhasaṃkhyāvidhiḥ smṛtaḥ
ardhacandraṃ ca candraṃ ca śakaṭaṃ makaraṃ tathā
kamalaṃ śreṇikāṃ gulmaṃ vyūhān evaṃ prakalpate
ye rājaputrāḥ sāmantāḥ āptāḥ sevakajātayaḥ
rakṣaṇīyāḥ prayatne na sadaiva hitakāriṇaḥ
yasmin raṇe yaḥ puruṣaḥ pradhānaḥ sa sarvayatnena hi rakṣaṇīyaḥ
tasmin vinaṣṭe kila sarvabhūte sarve'pi yodhāstvabalā bhavanti
saṃgrāmasārabhūtaṃ ca śāstrajñam anuvāsi cet
api ca svaśriyai sainyaṃ vṛtheyaṃ muṇḍamaṇḍalī
api pañcaśatāḥ śūrā gṛhṇanti mahatīṃ camūm
athavā pañca ṣaṭsapta vijayante 'nuvartinaḥ
dhanuḥsaṃhatiśuddhātmā rājāno mukhadurbalāḥ
ā karṇapalitā yodhāḥ saṃgrāme jayavādinaḥ
parasparānuraktā ye yodhāḥ śārṅgadhanurdharāḥ
yuddhajñāstu rathārūḍhāste jayanti raṇe ripūn
ekaḥ kāpuruṣādīrṇo dārayenmahatīṃ camūm
taddīrṇam anudīryante yodhāḥ śūratamā api
durnivāratayā caiva samagrāṃ mahatīṃ camūm
apāmiva mahāvegas trāsayet kuśalānapi
api bhagneṣu sainyeṣu vidruteṣu nivartate
pade pade'śvamedhasya labhate phalamakṣayam
dvāvimau puruṣau loke sūryamaṇḍalabhedinau
parivrāḍ yogayuktaśca raṇe cābhimukho hataḥ
yatra yatra hataḥ śūraḥ śatrubhiḥ pariveṣṭitaḥ
akṣayaṃ labhate lokaṃ yadi dainyaṃ na bhāṣate
mūrchitaṃ naiva vikalaṃ nāśastraṃ nānyayodhinam
palāyamāṇaṃ śaraṇaṃ gataṃ caiva na hiṃsayet
bhīruḥ palāyamāṇo'pi na hantavyo balīyasā
kadācicchūratāṃ yāti śaraṇe kṛtavismṛtaḥ
samprūjya mahatīṃ senāṃ caturaṅgāṃ mahīpatiḥ
vyūhayitvā parān śūrān sthāpayejjayalipsayā
alpāyāṃ vā mahatyāṃ vā senāyāmiti niścayaḥ
harṣo yodhagaṇasyāpi jayalakṣaṇam ucyate
anvevaṃ vāyavo yānti dakṣiṇe ca vayāṃsi ca
anusarve'pi piśitā yasya yānti raṇe jayaḥ
apūrṇaṃ caiva mantavyaṃ sampūrṇenaiva sidhyati
tasmāddhairyaṃ prakartavyaṃ hantavyā paravāhinī
jīvallakṣmī mṛte svargaḥ kīrtiśca dharaṇītale
tasmāddhairyaṃ vidhātavyaṃ hantavyā paravāhinī
iti śrīmatsadāśivaprokto dhanurvedaḥ samāptaḥ
