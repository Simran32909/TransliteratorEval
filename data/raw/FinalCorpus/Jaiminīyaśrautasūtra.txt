gharme sādyamāna ārūḍhavadāṅgirasaṃ trir gāyati kāvaṃ vā
vāmadevyena madantībhiḥ śāntiṃ kṛtvā yathāprapannaṃ niṣkramya saṃsthitāyām upasadyutkare tiṣṭhan subrahmaṇyāmāhūya yathārtham eti
atha parigāṇāni
na geyāni nādhvaryuḥ saṃpreṣyati yadādhvaryuḥ saṃpreṣyati geyānīti
sve vā yajñe gāyed yaṃ vā śreyāṃsaṃ kāmayeta tasya vā
dīkṣaṇīyāyāṃ tārkṣyasāmanī gāyet
prāyaṇīyāyāṃ pravadbhārgavam
ātithyāyām auśanaṃ preṣṭhaṃ vo atithim iti
pravargye ca yathāliṅgam upatiṣṭhet
agner vrataṃ somasya vrataṃ viṣṇor vratam ity upasatsu
sadā nimīyamāne sadasā
havirdhāne havirdhānābhyām
agniṃ praṇayanti tad agner vratam
agnīṣomau praṇayanti tad agner vrataṃ somasya caiva vrataṃ tṛtīyam
pavitre rājany ānīyamāne trīṇi somasāmāni gāyet somaḥ pavata iti yāni prathamād ūrdhvam
ahar ahaḥ satre satrasyarddhi gāyet
saṃsthite 'hani paścād gārhapatyaṃ yajñasārathy ahīnaikāheṣu ca
prākśvaḥsutyāṃ paścādeva gārhapatyam agner agastyasyātrer iti rākṣoghnāni sāmāni gāyet
sarvān saṃśrāvayed yajamānān sapatnīkān
anūbandhyeṣu ca yathāliṅgam
aukṣṇorandhram ukṣaṇi
ārṣabham ṛṣabhe
vāśaṃ vaśāyām
unnate traikakubham
ājīkam aje
paya āmikṣāyām
dadhini dadhikram
madhuni madhuścyunnidhanam
ājye ghṛtaścyunnidhanam
aurṇāyavam āvike meṣe ca
athodayanīyāyām udvat
udavasanīyāyām udvaṃśīyam
sautrāmaṇyāṃ surāyāṃ sāṃśānāni sāmāni gāyet
atha paśubandheṣu
sāvitrāṇi sāvitre
āgneyāny āgneye
aindrāṇy aindre
vaiśvadevāni vaiśvadeve
prājāpatyāni prājāpatye
yathāliṅgaṃ ca yathādaivataṃ cānyeṣu
teṣāṃ yāni paśau śiṣṭāni vapānte tāni gāyet
pradānakāla upasatsu ceṣṭiṣu ca
tāni tattatkarmāpanno yajñopavītī prāgāvṛttas tiṣṭhann upaviṣṭo vā madhyamayā vācā gāyet
teṣāṃ yāni tṛcasthāni tṛceṣu tāni gāyet
yāny ekarcāni tris tris tāni tṛcepsatāyai
tatra padāya padāya stobhān anusaṃhared ity ācāryasamayaḥ
yathādhītāny eva geyānīty anubrāhmaṇino vacanāt
tasmān mantraikadeśābhyāsaḥ syād udvāsanīya eva sarvo nidhanam upeyād avabhṛthasāmni ca
na vārṣāhare
āvartivrataśukriyeṣu catuḥkarmāpannāḥ kuryuḥ
bhrājābhrājābhyāṃ tūpadravanidhane trir ukte syātām
kasya hetor iti
ekaviṃśe bhavataḥ
sarvam āvarti pañcoktaṃ mahāvrate
anyatra prakṣāvebhyaḥ prakṣāvebhyaḥ
