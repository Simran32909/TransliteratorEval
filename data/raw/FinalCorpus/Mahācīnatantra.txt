devy uvāca
devadeva mahādeva mahākāruṇika prabho
yoginām kiṃ sadā sevyam kaulānāṃ varapuṣṭidam
manaścāñcalyaharaṇam kiṃ samādhiphalapradam
vadasva tad aśeṣeṇa yady ahaṃ tava vallabhā
śiva uvāca
akathyam etat cārvaṅgi devāsuranareṣv api
kathayāmi tava prītyā guhyād guhyataram mahat
śrutvā tvayāpi goptavyam yatnena paśusaṃkaṭe
valo nāma mahān āsīd asuro balavān purā
jitvā suragaṇān sarvān indro 'bhūt svayam eva saḥ
tataḥ palāyitāḥ sarve bhayodvignā divaukasaḥ
puraṃdaram purodhāya mantrayitvā suniścitam
nānyo 'sti tasmād asmākam tataḥ śrīmadhusūdanāt
iti saṃcintya te sarve prayayuḥ parameśvaram
saṃstūyamānam ṛṣibhiḥ siddhakiṃnarasevitam
taṃ dṛṣṭvā puṇḍarikākṣaṃ praṇamya daṇḍavad bhuvi
ūcuḥ prāñjalayaścaite devāḥ śakrapurogamāḥ
namo 'stu haraye tubhyam sarvaduḥkhaharāya ca
surāṇām ārtināśāya sarveśāya namo namaḥ
namas trimūrtaye tubhyam sṛṣṭisthityantakāriṇe
guṇātītāya śuddhāya parato 'pi parāya ca
sarvātmane namas te 'stu sarvaśaktiyutāya te
sarvajñāya sarvalokasākṣiṇe te namo namaḥ
iti stutvā ca sakalam tad vṛttāntam nyavedayan
śaraṇam tvām gatāḥ sarve trāhi naś caiva nityaśaḥ
durācāro valo mṛtyum yathā yāty acireṇa saḥ
rakṣāsmākaṃ ca śakrasya tathā kartuṃ tvam arhasi
śrutvedam vacanam teṣām idam āha puraṃdaram
vṛttāntam etaj jānāmi valasyāsya durātmanaḥ
brahmaṇā na mayā vāpi na sa vadhyaḥ śivena vā
tvayaiva vadhyo bhavitā sa durātmā na saṃśayaḥ
etat sarvam maheśāya nivedaya puraṃdara
sa eva bhagavān sarvam etaj jānāti śaṃkaraḥ
tavābhilaṣitaṃ sarvam sa vidhāsyati gamyatām
prasādyatām ca yatnena tvayā saubhaktavatsalaḥ
atha praṇamya śakras tam samāyāto mamāntikam
āgatya śuddhabhāvo 'sau praṇamya ca kṛtāñjaliḥ
tuṣṭāva vividhaiḥ stotrair bhaktigadgadabhāṣitaiḥ
kailāse nivasantam māṃ dhyānoparatam īśvaram
stotrapāṭhair bahuvidhais tadudbhavanināditaiḥ
mahadbhiḥ parito jñānaṃ sarvaṃ tadavaśeṣataḥ
asyaiva vadhyaḥ sa valo durātmāsau bhaviṣyati
bhaviṣyam etad vijñāya śakram āhūya saṃnidhim
tasmai tat paramaṃ jñānam dattvāvocam idaṃ vacaḥ
trailokyavijayājñānam etat trailokyadurlabham
trailokyavijayājñānāj jñānaṃ nāsti maheśvari
aśeṣajāḍyaharaṇaṃ paramātmaprakāśanam
akhaṇḍānandanilayam advaitaṃ paramaṃ padam
manaḥsthairyakaraṃ rogaśokasaṃtāpanāśanam
balapuṣṭikaram samyagyogasiddhikaraṃ param
niṣevyatām iyaṃ saṃvit sthīyatām ca mamāntike
tato gatvā vatsarānte sa valo jeṣyate tvayā
ity evam uktaḥ sa mayā prāñjaliś cedam abravīt
śakra uvāca
devadeva mahādeva sarvajña sarvatattvavit
vidhinā kena sevyeyaṃ ko vā mantro viśodhane
tarpaṇe svīkṛtau mantraḥ saṃvidaḥ kṛpayā vibho
sarvam etad aśeṣeṇa sāṅgopāṅgam vadasva me
śrīśiva uvāca
śṛṇu śakra mahābhāga sarvam etad vadāmi te
śrutvā gopaya yatnena tad evācara nityaśaḥ
trailokyavijayākhyāyā nāmāni prathamaṃ kramāt
kathayāmi tava snehāt atiguhyāni sarvataḥ
trailokyavijayā siddhā siddhidā siddhimūlikā
jñānam saṃvit saṃvidā ca śyāmā bhaṅgā rasāyanā
yogadā yoginām sevyā yogamārgaprakāśinī
brāhmaṇī brahmasambhūtā brahmānandapradāyinī
vāgdevatā vaśakarī vijayā vijayapradā
samādhivaradā jñānanāśinī caikaviṃśatiḥ
śvetaraktapītakṛṣṇapuṣpabhedāccaturvidhā
śvetapuṣpā brāhmaṇī sā kṣatriyā raktapuṣpikā
pītapuṣpā tu vaiśyā ca śūdrā tu kṛṣṇapuṣpikā
ākṛṣya pattrāṇy etāsām sabījāni prayatnataḥ
bharjitāni ghṛtenaiva cūrṇitāni śilātale
trikaṭutriphalāśṛṅgī kuṣṭham dhanyākasaindhavam
śaṭī tālīśapatraṃ ca kaṭphalaṃ nāgakesaram
yamānīdvayam methī ca jīrakadvayam eva ca
etāni samabhāgāni raudraśuṣkāni cūrṇayet
gṛhītvā cūrṇam etat tu saṃviccūrṇaṃ tu tatsamam
ubhayos tu samam kṛtvā sitāṃ saṃmiśrayed ghṛte
miśrayitvā caturthāṃśam samaṃ madhu ghṛtam tathā
punaḥ śilātale piṣṭvā triṣu gandham pravartayet
svarṇādipātre purataḥ sthāpayitvā tataḥ kramāt
