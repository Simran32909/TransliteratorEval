aviśeṣartukālena sarve nirghātādayaḥ smṛtāḥ
yac cānyad daivam adbhutaṃ sarvaṃ nirghātavad bhavet
ṛtāv adhyāyaś chāndasaḥ kālpya āpartukaḥ smṛtaḥ
ṛtāv ūrdhvaṃ prātarāśād yas tu kaścid anadhyāyaḥ
saṃdhyāṃ prāpnoti paścimām
sarveṇa pradoṣo lupyate
niśi nigadāyāṃ ca vidyuti śiṣṭaṃ nādhīyīta
astamite dvisattāyāṃ trisattāyāṃ ca pāṭavaḥ
atha tāvatkālaṃ bhuktvā pradoṣa ubhe saṃdhye
apsu śmaśāne śayyāyām abhiśaste khileṣu ca
antaḥśave rathyāyāṃ grāme cāṇḍālasaṃyute
durgandhe śūdrasaṃśrāve paiṅge śabde bhaye rute
vaidhṛtye nagareṣu ca
aniktena ca vāsasā caritaṃ yena maithunam
śayānaḥ prauḍhapādo cāgratopasthāntike guroḥ
viramya mārute śīghre pratyārambho vibhāṣitaḥ
sarveṇāpararātreṇa viramya pratyārambho na vidyate
pauṣī pramāṇam abhreṣv āpartu ced adhīyānām
varṣaṃ vidyut stanayitnur vā vipadyate
trirātraṃ sthānāsanaṃ brahmacaryam arasāśaṃ copayeyuḥ
sā tatra prāyaścittiḥ sā tatra prāyaścittiḥ
