jāraṇāṃ vivakṣuḥ prathamaṃ tadbhedān āha varavārtikaiḥ śreṣṭharasaśāstratattvajñair iti trirūpā tribhedā jāraṇā nirdiṣṭā proktā
kāni trīṇi rūpāṇi garbhe cāraṇaṃ garbhe drāvaṇaṃ garbhe jāraṇam
garbhaśabdaḥ sarvatra sambadhyate
garbhacāraṇaṃ garbhadrāvaṇaṃ garbhajāraṇaṃ ceti bhāvaḥ
punar bhūyo jāraṇā iti dvividhā dviprakārā proktā kathitā
kiṃ taddvaividhyaṃ nirmukhā samukhā ca
nirmukhajāraṇā samukhajāraṇā ceti yāvat
jāraṇālakṣaṇaṃ yathā drutagrāsaparīṇāmo viḍayantrādiyogataḥ jāraṇetyucyate tasyāḥ prakārāḥ santi koṭiśaḥ iti
