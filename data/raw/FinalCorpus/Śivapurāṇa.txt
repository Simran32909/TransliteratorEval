māhātmyametadvṛṣabhadhvajasya śrutvā muner gandhavatīsutasya
vaco mahārthaṃ munayaśca sarve papracchur ugraṃ praṇipatya sūtam
munaya ūcuḥ
kimandhakārirbhagavān pinākī kasyānvaye vīryavataḥ pṛthivyām
jāto mahātmā balavān pradhāne kaścāndhakaḥ kasya sutaḥ kathaṃ vā
etat samagraṃ sarahasyamadya bravīhi sūta bhagavatprasādāt
vyāsāt tvayā vai viditaṃ hi samyag yuddhaṃ yathābhūd anayor hataḥ saḥ
teṣāṃ tadāsau vacanaṃ niśamya provāca sūtastu munīṃstadānīm
pūrvaṃ dvijā mandaraśailasaṃsthā kaparddinaścaṇḍaparākramasya
cakre tato netranimīlanaṃ tu sā pārvvatī narmayutaṃ salīlam
pravālahemābjadhṛtaprabhābhyāṃ karāmbujābhyāṃ nimimīla netre
harasya netreṣu nimīliteṣu kṣaṇena jātaḥ sumahāndhakāraḥ
tatsparśayogācca maheśvarasya karācca tasyāḥ skhalitaṃ madāmbhaḥ
śambhorlalāṭekṣaṇavahnitapto vinirgato bhūrijalasya binduḥ
garbho babhūvātha karānanasya bhayaṅkaraḥ krodhaparaḥ kṛtaghnaḥ
anyo virūpī jaṭilaḥ śmaśrulaśca kṛṣṇo naro vai vikṛtaḥ suromā
gāyan hasan prarudan nṛtyamāno vilelihāno ghanaghoraghoṣaḥ
jātena tenādbhutadarśanena gaurīṃ bhavo'sau smitapūrvam āha
nimīlya netrāṇi kṛtaṃ ca narma vibheṣase māṃ dayite kathaṃ tvam
gaurī harāt tadvacanaṃ niśamya vihasyamānā pramumoca netre
jāte prakāśe sati ghorarūpo jāto'ndhakārādapi netrahīnaḥ
papraccha ko'yaṃ bhagavan virūpas tādṛgvidhaṃ tacca nirīkṣya bhūtam
vadasva satyaṃ mama kiṃ nimittaṃ sṛṣṭo'thavā kena ca kasya puttraḥ
śrutvā harastadvacanaṃ priyāyā utpanna eṣo'dbhutacaṇḍavīryyaḥ
nimīlite cakṣuṣī me bhavatyā sa svedajaścāndhakanāmadheyaḥ
tato'sya kartāsmi yathānurūpaṃ tvayā sasakhyā sadayaṃ gaṇebhyaḥ
sa rakṣitavyas tvayanaṃ hi caikaṃ vicārya buddhyā karaṇīyam ārye
gaurī tato bharttṛvaco niśamya kāruṇyabhāvāt sahitā sakhībhiḥ
nānāprakārair bahubhir hyupāyaiścakāra rakṣāṃ svasutasya yadvat
kāle'tha yasmin bhuvi cāndhakastu hiraṇyanetrastvatha putrakāmaḥ
araṇyam āśritya tapaścakāra jātastadā kaśyapajaḥ sutārtham
kāṣṭhopamo'sau jitaroṣadoṣaḥ saṃdarśanārthaṃ tu maheśvarasya
tuṣṭaḥ pinākī tapasāsya samanyag varapradānāya yayau dvijendrāḥ
tuṣṭastamāhākulitendriyaṃ taṃ kimartham etadvratam āśritaṃ te
prabrūhi kāmaṃ varado bhavo'haṃ yad icchasi tvaṃ sakalaṃ dadāmi
puttrastu me candralalāṭa nāsti suvīryavān daityakulānurūpī
tadarthametad vratam āsthito'haṃ taṃ dehi deveśa suvīryyavantam
yasmācca madbhrāturanantavīryyāḥ prahrādapūrvā api pañca puttrāḥ
mameha nāstīti niranvayo'haṃ yo māmakaṃ rājyamidaṃ bubhūṣet
rāṣṭraṃ parasya svabalena hṛtvā bhuṅkte'thavā svaṃ pitureva diṣṭam
sa procyate puttra ihāpyamutra puttrī sa tenāpi bhavet pitāsau
ūrdhvaṃ gatiḥ puttravatāṃ niruktā manīṣibhir dharmavatāṃ variṣṭhaiḥ
sarvāṇi bhūtāni tadarthameva mṛtau pravartanti haṭhāt svabhāvataḥ
niranvayasyāpi na santi lokās tadarthamicchanti sutān svabhūmne
krītādayaḥ santi sutāstathāṣṭau icchāmi tasmāt sutam ekameva
etadbhavastadvacanaṃ niśamya kṛpākaro daityanṛpasya tuṣṭaḥ
tamāha daityādhipa nāsti puttras tvadvīryyajaḥ kiṃtu dadāmi puttram
mamātmajaṃ tvandhakanāmadheyaṃ tvattulyavīryyaṃ hyaparājitaṃ ca
vṛṇīṣva puttraṃ sakalaṃ vihāya duḥkhaṃ pratīcchasva sukhaṃ hi cemam
ityevamuktvā pradadau sa tasmai hiraṇyanetrāya sutaṃ prasannaḥ
harastu gauryā sahito mahātmā bhūtādhināthastripurārirugraḥ
tato harāt prāpya sutaṃ sa daityaḥ pradakṣiṇīkṛtya yathākrameṇa
stotrair anekairabhipūjya rudraṃ hṛṣṭaḥ svarājyaṃ gatavān mahātmā
tatastu puttraṃ giriśād avāpya rasātalaṃ caṇḍaparākramastu
imāṃ dharitrīmanayat svadeśaṃ daityo vijitya tridaśānaśeṣān
tatastu devairmunibhiśca sarvaiḥ sarvātmakaṃ yajñamayaṃ karālam
vārāhamāśritya vapuḥ pradhānam ārādhito viṣṇur anantavīryyaḥ
ghoṇāprahārair vividhairdharitrīṃ vidārya pātālatalaṃ praviśya
tuṇḍena daityān śataśo vicūrṇya daṃṣṭrābhir agryābhir akhaṇḍitābhiḥ
pādaprahārair aśaniprakāśair unmathya sainyāni niśācarāṇām
mārtaṇḍakoṭipratimena paścāt sudarśanenādbhutacaṇḍatejāḥ
hiraṇyanetrasya śiro jvalantaṃ cicheda daityāṃśca dadāha duṣṭān
tataḥ prahṛṣṭo ditijendrarājye tamandhakaṃ tatra sa cābhyaṣiñcat
svasthānam āsādya tato dharitrīṃ daṃṣṭrāṅkureṇādadharat prahṛṣṭaḥ
bhūmiṃ ca pātālatalānmahātmā pupoṣa bhāgaṃ tvatha pūrvakaṃ tu
pratāpya lokānakhilāṃstato'sau samāgataḥ padmayoniṃ dadarśa
varaṃ pradātuṃ pravadantameva varaṃ vṛṇīṣveti sa mūḍhabuddhiḥ
mṛtyor bhayaṃ me bhagavan sadaiva pitāmahābhūnna kadācidevam
śastrāstrapāśāśaniśuṣkavṛkṣagirīndratoyāgniripuprahāraiḥ
svarge dharaṇyāṃ divase niśāyāṃ daivāhataḥ prāha na so'pi sandhau
tasyaitadīdṛgvacanaṃ niśamya daityendra tuṣṭo'smi labhasva sarvam
praṇamya viṣṇuṃ manasā tamāha bhayānvito'sāvapi padmayoniḥ
tataḥ sa daityaḥ paripūrṇakāmastvam aṣṭakoṭyastvatha ṣaṇṇavatyaḥ
uttiṣṭha rājyaṃ kuru dānavānāṃ śrutvā giraṃ tāṃ sa sukhī babhūva
rājye'bhiṣiktaḥ prapitāmahena trailokyanāśāya matiṃ cakāra
utsādya dharmmān sakalān pramatto jitvāhave so'pi surān samastān
tato bhayād indramukhāśca devāḥ kṣīrodadhau yatra haristu śete
ārādhayāmāsur atīva gatvā tatastu hṛṣṭaḥ pradadau varāṃstu
utthāya tasmācchayanād upendro yathānurūpair vividhair vacobhiḥ
āśvāsya devānakhilān munīndrā ravīnduvaiśvānaratulyatejāḥ
āśritya rūpaṃ jaṭilaṃ karālaṃ daṃṣṭrāyudhaṃ tīkṣṇanakhaṃ sunāsam
mṛgendratulyaṃ ca vidāritāsyaṃ mārtaṇḍakoṭipratimaṃ sughoram
yugāntakālāgnisamaprabhāvaṃ jaganmayaṃ kiṃ bahubhirvacobhiḥ
astaṃ ravau so'pi hi gacchamāne gato'surāṇāṃ nagarīṃ mahātmā
kṛtvā ca yuddhaṃ prabalaiḥ sahāyair hatvātha tān daityagaṇān grahītum
