ha atha atyāśramibhyaḥ paramam pavitram provāca samyak ṛṣi saṃgha juṣṭam
vedānte paramam guhyam purākalpe pracoditam
na a praśāntāya dātavyam na a putrāya a śiṣyāya _ punar
yasya deve parā bhaktiḥ yathā deve tathā gurau tasya ete kathitāḥ hi arthāḥ prakāśante mahātmanaḥ
