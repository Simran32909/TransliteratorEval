mahāsāhasavṛttyā svarūpalābhaḥ
tallābhāc churitā yudyugapad vṛttipravṛttiḥ
ubhayapaṭṭodghaṭṭanān mahāśūnyatāpraveśaḥ
yugmagrāsān niravakāśasaṃvinniṣṭhā
siddhayoginīsaṃghaṭṭān mahāmelāpodayaḥ
trikañcukaparityāgān nirākhyapadāvasthitiḥ
vākcatuṣṭayodayavirāmaprathāsu svaraḥ prathate
rasatritayāsvādanenānicchocchalitaṃ vigatabandhaṃ paraṃ brahma
devīcatuṣṭayollāsena sadaiva svaviśrāntyavasthitiḥ
dvādaśavāhodayena mahāmarīcivikāsaḥ
caryāpañcakodaye nistaraṅgasamāveśaḥ
mahābodhasamāveśāt puṇyapāpāsaṃbandhaḥ
akathanakathābalena mahāvismayamudrāprāptyā khasvaratā
